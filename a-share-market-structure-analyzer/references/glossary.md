# Glossary

## Core Terms

- 阶段: The current structural market state; classify before interpreting indicators.
- 核心防守区: Area where trend/chip defense should appear.
- 修复压力区: Area that price must reclaim for initial repair.
- 趋势确认区: Area that confirms the trend repair or upgrade.
- 强压力区: Area with strong supply/profit pressure.
- 失效条件: Conditions that invalidate the current constructive interpretation.
- 资金承接: Evidence that selling is being absorbed; not proof of institutional buying.
- 高位分歧: High-position disagreement after an advance.
- 高位派发: High-position distribution risk after core chip/trend support fails.
- 筹码集中度: Lower number means more concentrated chips; meaning changes by stage.
- 平均成本: Average chip cost shown by software or provided data.
- 70%成本区: Price interval containing 70% chip cost distribution.
- 90%成本区: Price interval containing 90% chip cost distribution.
- 减压线: A rebound/repair area where holders can reduce psychological or position pressure.
- 解套线: Breakeven area based on holder cost.

## Forbidden To Preferred

| Avoid | Prefer |
| --- | --- |
| 铁底 | 核心防守区 |
| 必涨/必跌 | 修复条件/失效条件 |
| 主力一定护盘 | 资金仍有承接 |
| 机构一定低吸 | 存在低吸或防守可能 |
| 融资买入就是利好 | 杠杆资金参与，需结合位置判断 |
| 死叉必跌 | 短线动能转弱，需结合阶段判断 |
| 金叉必涨 | 动能改善，仍需价格与OBV确认 |
