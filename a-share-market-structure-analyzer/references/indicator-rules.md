# Indicator Rules

## Moving Averages

Watch MA5, MA10, MA20, MA30, and MA60.

| State | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| MA5 > MA10 > MA20 > MA30 > MA60 | Strong bullish stack |
| Price breaks MA5/MA10 | Short-term attack weakens |
| Price nears MA20 | Trend life-line test |
| Breaks MA20 and cannot reclaim | Main-uptrend downgrade |
| Breaks MA30 | Intermediate adjustment |
| Breaks MA60 | Medium-term trend weakens |

Fixed wording:

- `MA5/MA10代表短线进攻`
- `MA20代表趋势生命线`
- `MA30代表中期支撑`
- `MA60代表长趋势背景`

## MACD

Analyze DIF, DEA, histogram bar, golden/death cross, red/green bars, bar expansion/shrinkage, zero-axis position, and divergence.

| State | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Golden cross with expanding red bars | Momentum strengthens |
| Death cross with green bars | Short-term momentum weakens |
| Green bars expanding | Adjustment deepens |
| Green bars shrinking | Downside momentum decays |
| Death cross above zero | Strong trend cool-down |
| Death cross below zero | Weak trend continues |
| Price new high while DIF fails new high | Top divergence risk |
| Price new low while DIF fails new low | Bottom divergence clue |

Principle: MACD death cross is not automatically a sell signal. It must be read with stage, chips, OBV, and key zones.

## OBV

Use OBV to judge fund confirmation and divergence, without overclaiming.

| State | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Price new high + OBV new high | Rise has fund confirmation |
| Price new high + OBV no new high | Top divergence risk |
| Price falls + OBV does not collapse | There is acceptance; cannot directly call distribution |
| Price breaks support + OBV turns down | Risk upgrades |
| OBV high-position sideways | Fund disagreement |
| OBV continues down | Capital withdrawal |

Hard rule: never say `OBV上涨一定是机构低吸`. Use `资金仍有承接` or `存在低吸或防守可能`.

## Volume And Turnover

| Volume/Price Structure | Judgment |
| --- | --- |
| Shrinking-volume rise | Strong lock-up |
| Rising with volume expansion | Healthy attack |
| Volume expansion without price progress | Distribution risk |
| High-volume long bearish candle | High risk |
| Shrinking-volume pullback | Potential healthy adjustment |
| High-turnover rise | Disagreement becomes agreement |
| High-turnover sideways | High-position contest |
| High-turnover fall | Capital withdrawal risk |

## Combination Rules

- Trend remains acceptable when price tests MA20/70% lower edge while OBV stays stable and volume shrinks.
- Risk upgrades when price breaks MA20 or 70% lower edge with expanding green MACD bars and OBV turning down.
- Repair starts when price regains average cost/MA5/MA10 and MACD green bars shrink or turn red.
- Confirmation needs price reclaiming key zone plus OBV repair; a price rebound alone is weak.
