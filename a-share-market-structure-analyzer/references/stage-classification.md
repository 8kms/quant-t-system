# Stage Classification

Classify the stage first. Indicator meaning depends on stage.

## Stage Table

| Stage | Core Features | Interpretation | Discipline |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 底部吸筹型 | Low long sideways range; concentration improves; OBV slowly rises; volume is mild; MACD repeats crosses; price oscillates around MA20/MA60 | Funds may be collecting, but main uptrend is unconfirmed | Do not chase; wait for volume breakout, OBV new high, and chip peak rising |
| 底部修复型 | Rebounds from low; regains MA20/MA30; MACD golden cross; OBV rises; chip peak starts moving up | Repair is active, but not yet main uptrend | Observe MA20 pullback; beware falling below average cost; upgrade only after previous high breakout |
| 横盘蓄势型 | MAs converge; price oscillates near average cost; concentration improves; MACD dulls; OBV flat or slowly rising | Long/short balance, waiting for direction | Upward breakout needs volume; downward break needs OBV check; do not pre-assume direction |
| 主升启动型 | Volume breakout; MA5 > MA10 > MA20; MACD red bars expand; OBV new high; chip peak rises | Trend enters attack state | Watch pullback confirmation; shrinking pullback above platform is stronger; volume drop back into platform is false-break risk |
| 主升加速型 | Consecutive strong candles; price far from MA20; OBV quickly reaches new high; high-position MACD expansion; turnover rises but not out of control | Strongest stage, but chasing risk expands | Hold strong with acceptance; avoid blind chase after acceleration; first high-volume long bearish candle is critical |
| 高位分歧型 | High-position volume drop; breaks MA5/MA10; MACD death cross/green bars; OBV pulls back but does not collapse; still inside 70% chip zone | Short-term attack ends; medium trend is under test | Watch MA20 and 70% lower edge; regaining average cost is initial repair; breaking 70% lower edge upgrades risk |
| 高位派发型 | Breaks 70% lower edge; OBV turns down; MACD green bars expand; rebound fails average cost; volume remains large; concentration worsens | Stop treating as simple wash; handle as capital withdrawal risk | Rebounds are pressure-release chances; do not keep old support as support; prevent high-position correction from becoming intermediate decline |
| 反弹失败型 | Rebound meets pressure; cannot break average cost/MA20/MA30; MACD death cross; OBV stops making new highs; price falls back below cost zone | Prior rebound may be weak repair only | Wait for reclaiming average cost; breaking core cost lower edge confirms failure |
| 破位下行型 | Breaks 90% lower edge; MA20/MA30/MA60 all pressure price; OBV keeps falling; MACD green bars expand below zero; rebound lacks volume | Chip and trend structure both deteriorate | Prioritize risk control; do not buy just because it fell a lot; wait for base-building |

## Classification Hints

- If current price is in the 70% chip zone after a high-volume high-position drop, default to `高位分歧型` until 70% lower edge breaks or repair confirms.
- If price breaks 70% lower edge and OBV turns down together, upgrade to `高位派发型`.
- If price breaks 90% lower edge and long MAs press down, classify as `破位下行型`.
- If MAs converge around average cost and chips tighten, classify as `横盘蓄势型`.
- If a low rebound regains MA20/MA30 and OBV rises, classify as `底部修复型`.
