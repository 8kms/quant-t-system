# Chip Distribution Rules

Chip data should come from visible screenshot data or trusted software/export. If missing, mark it missing.

## Required Chip Fields

- Average cost.
- 70% cost zone.
- 90% cost zone.
- 70% concentration.
- 90% concentration.
- Profit ratio.
- Trapped ratio.
- Chip peak position.
- Chip peak movement direction.
- Current price relative to chip zones.

## Concentration

Core definition: lower concentration number means chips are more concentrated. Its meaning changes by stage.

| Stage | Lower Concentration Means |
| --- | --- |
| 底部横盘 | Positive: accumulation/cost convergence |
| 主升中继 | Positive: sufficient turnover and stronger lock-up |
| 高位震荡 | Neutral: may be control or concentrated risk |
| 高位下跌 | Risk: break may trigger concentrated selling |
| 破位下行 | Dangerous: concentrated losing chips may stop out together |

## Price Relative To Chip Zone

| Current Price Position | Judgment |
| --- | --- |
| Above 90% cost upper edge | Extremely strong but possibly overheated |
| Above 70% cost upper edge | Relatively strong |
| Inside 70% cost zone | Core contest zone |
| Below average cost | Trapped pressure increases |
| Below 70% cost lower edge | Core chip zone lost |
| Below 90% cost lower edge | Chip structure damaged |

## Profit And Trapped Chips

- High profit ratio after acceleration means potential profit-taking pressure; watch high-volume long bearish candles.
- High trapped ratio below average cost means every rebound may meet sell pressure.
- If price reclaims average cost with OBV repair, trapped pressure begins to release.
- If price stays below average cost and volume expands, treat rebounds as pressure-release unless repair confirms.

## Chip Peak Movement

- Chip peak rising with price and OBV new highs supports trend upgrading.
- Chip peak staying low while price rises may imply unresolved trapped pressure.
- Chip peak moving downward during decline means cost center is deteriorating.
- A high-position single dense chip peak is not automatically bullish; if broken, selling pressure may concentrate.

## Fixed Expressions

- `当前价仍在70%成本区内，说明核心筹码区尚未完全失守。`
- `跌破70%成本下沿后，不能再简单按洗盘处理。`
- `平均成本上方是修复确认，不是天然安全。`
- `集中度改善要结合阶段判断，不能单独视为利好。`
